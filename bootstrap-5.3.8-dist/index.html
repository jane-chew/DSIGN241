The Problem & Our Solution

The Problem

University students often struggle to find compatible peers to study with, especially outside of their immediate friend groups or classes. While many students benefit from collaborative learning, there is no simple, centralized way to discover and connect with others who share the same courses, schedules, goals, or study preferences.

Our Solution

Peer Study Match is a website designed for students in New Zealand, with regional access options based on either their institution or broader student communities. The platform can be hosted on a private university server or accessed through a public student server varying between university portals to connect students for short study or long period sessions.
